Mission & Goals

The Robotics Club meets every Wednesday at 11:00 am - 12:00 pm in room BUDD 221 G-2. Professor Sedghi is the adviser. The Robotics Club holds meetings to discuss robotics, the trends that are occurring in the robotics industry and collaborate to work on projects.

We are a group that is excited to build physical things and expand the curiosity of themselves and others. Our mission is to push forward whats possible as a student organisation, both in terms of technical accomplishments and educational value. That is why we welcome students from all backgrounds and experiences, and offer them real hands on experience in constructing robots and leading a team. Our ultimate goal is to open the possibility of becoming a robotics innovator to anyone.
